Tempo Typer: Type to the Beat!

Tempo Typer is a combination of two genres I haven't seen together before, a typing game and a rhythm game.




Rather than trying to type as fast as possible, Tempo Typer challenges you to try typing at the BPM of the song, which is a lot different to get used to than you might think.




I used music from TheFatRat's website called The Arcadium which had a great selection of songs for use.
